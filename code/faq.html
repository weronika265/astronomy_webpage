FAQ

Who are we?

We are the group of people who want to show the subject of space
in our own different, yet unique way.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to spread the knowlage of the astronomy in the easiest way
possible, so anyone can learn about the beautiful world surrounding us.

The team

Our team consists of people of different cultures and races - we are
the team of very diverse people, just like the stars.

Can anyone join the team?

Of course! You can also feel free to join, we will be more than happy to
welcome you in our ranks. Just complete the form on this page.

Where can I get your products?

Currently, only in our local shop. Still, we are trying our best to make our
products be available worldwide.
